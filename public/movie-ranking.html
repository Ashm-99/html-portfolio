Best Movies According to Ashmeet

My top 3 Favorite Movies Of All Time

Tamasha

One of the best acted and directed indian movie of all time. Tells us to follow our heart and be happy in your life and work.

The Batman

Robert Pattison fits perfectly as a batman. A very good interpretation of Batman comics and animated series.

Tangled

Modern take on Rapunzel. Fun to watch with added twist of magic hair.
